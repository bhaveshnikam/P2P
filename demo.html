About eVote

Revolutionizing online elections since 2003

eVote is an election system that facilitates voters to record their secure and secret ballot electronically. It has a friendly user interface and enables voters to cast their votes in few simple steps. We ensures the authenticity of the voters and the votes cast by them along with non-traceability of the casted vote. eVote's robust architecture has persistently manifested to be one of the most reliable, comprehensible and economical electronic voting solution.

It renders Simple and Accessible voter experience that eventually increases voter engagement and turnout. Auditable, Easy To Use, Secure and Reliable is what sets eVote apart from its competitors.

eVote has facilitated several organizations, across a wide range of industries to conduct hassle-free electronic voting with utmost security and integrity. Some of our fortes include outstanding and prompt customer support, highly secure and trustworthy elections and last but not the least; our potential to be able to tabulate expeditious and accurate results.

We’d welcome the opportunity to work with you to provide exceptional and unparalleled service and add you to our ever-growing list of satisfied clients.

How it works

Conducting online election on eVote is a simple 3 step process.

Set-up Election

On successful registration, with our effortless ballot setup, you can set-up election in no time. This process involves 3 steps:

ADD ELECTION DETAILS

Add election details like election date and time, candidate’s names and photos.
ADD VOTER LIST

Drag and drop to import the list of voters from our convenient spreadsheet template.
REVIEW & LAUNCH

Once you've entered all the required details, review it and then you are good to go. Schedule a start/end date or immediately launch the election.

Vote

Broadcast SMS and E-mails are sent to your voters to notify them about the election link, clicking on which voters can record their vote through any internet-enabled device.

Results

After the election ends, on approval of election officer, the result can be generated which is automatically tabulated. This is solely for security purposes. The outcome can be published and shared with the voters on your eVote website.

Go Green

‘Go green’ is a phrase that is reiterated time and again in the prevalent business environment, to encourage businesses to mitigate its carbon emissions from their day-to-day business activities. ‘Greening’ of business activities is a dedicated and smart way of conducting business, with the intent to protect the environment and preserve natural resources.

Conforming to this thought we, the developers of eVote have gone green or made proactive efforts to endorse different forms of eco-friendly business initiatives. We have done our bit to conserve the environment through automation of the voting process and bringing in more efficient technologies and processes to enable energy and resource efficiency.

Every ton of paper destroys 12 trees. With eVote there is zero paper wastage, as it entails you to continue using your systems or mobile phones to record your vote. Everything from candidate's profile to ballots and result tabulation is online and paperless. Additionally, the servers that power eVote use thermal, hydro and non-conventional sources like solar, wind, etc. This causes a drastic reduction in CO2 output by power source. We ensure that the power we use for our servers come from clean, renewable sources, displacing energy from polluting sources.

Besides, our servers where the data is stored are virtualized or cloud-based. Studies show that cloud computing can help organizations to reduce the energy use and carbon footprint by up to 90 percent!

By choosing eVote you not only bring down your organization’s carbon emissions footprint considerably but also make your business more efficient, and cost-effective.
